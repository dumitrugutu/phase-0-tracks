-- Washing dishes --

- Locate the sink.
- If sink not located, exit the bedroom.
- When sink is located, find the faucet.
- When faucet is located, twist the knob.
    - If knob not found, look for lever. 
    - When lever located, lift lever.
    - Wait until the water starts running.
    - Pick up sponge and a plate.
    - Place under running water and gently rub plate's surface
    with sponge until clean.
    - Flip plate to opposite side and repeat action.
    - When done, place plate in the drain basket.
    - Repeat action for each plate until nothing left in the sink.
    - When done, stop the the running water.
- Stop!
- Tell wife.
